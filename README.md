# Project Veyra

**v0.0.1 — DEMO Test**

An AI-native MMORPG where NPCs remember your story.

## The World

Project Veyra is a persistent online RPG built on 0G infrastructure. Unlike traditional MMORPGs that track your stats, Project Veyra tracks your *story*. Every conversation, every gift, every choice — remembered permanently on decentralized storage.

## Meet Lyra

At the heart of Lunaris Village lives Lyra, a merchant NPC powered by AI. She doesn't repeat canned dialogue. She remembers you. Talk to her, bring her gifts, complete her quests — and your relationship deepens through a system called **Resonance**.

Resonance levels:
- **Stranger** (0–20) — Polite but distant
- **Friend** (21–40) — Warm, shares local lore
- **Companion** (41–60) — Trusts you, better deals
- **Beloved** (61–80) — Personal stories, exclusive quests
- **Soulbond** (81–100) — Permanent bond, marketplace perks

## Economy

Three layers of currency drive the world:

| Currency | How You Earn It | What It's For |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Gold** | Killing monsters, selling loot | Basic gear, potions |
| **Veyra Shards (VS)** | Quests, rare drops | Premium items, marketplace |
| **VEYA Token** | Converted from VS (100:1) | On-chain settlement, Soulbond |

All marketplace transactions are settled on-chain. Soulbonded players pay reduced tax (2% vs 5%).

## Combat

Real-time combat against creatures of the Azure Coast. Defeat Tide Slimes, Sand Crabs, Reef Serpents, and the fearsome Coral Ravager. Earn EXP, level up, collect rare drops.

## 0G Integration

Every meaningful action in Project Veyra touches 0G infrastructure:

- **Lyra's Memory** — Stored on 0G Storage. Permanent. Immutable. She never forgets.
- **World Chronicle** — Every player action recorded on-chain. Your story becomes part of Veyra's history.
- **Marketplace Settlement** — Trades verified on 0G Chain.
- **Soulbond NFT** — The bond between player and Lyra, minted on-chain.

## Built For

0G Zero Cup Hackathon — proving that AI-native games need decentralized storage, not just another smart contract.
